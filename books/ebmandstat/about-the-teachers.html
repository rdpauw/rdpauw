About the teachers

De lessen binnen het vak worden georganiseerd door dr. Robby De Pauw en prof. dr. Pascal Coorevits. Vragen kunnen steeds gepost worden in de discussieruimtes op Ufora. You can install both programs via the Sciensano software center. This is however not recommended because it will instalable through the software center is not always the most recent one. We therefore recommend to download the corresponding websites and install them on your C:\-drive.
